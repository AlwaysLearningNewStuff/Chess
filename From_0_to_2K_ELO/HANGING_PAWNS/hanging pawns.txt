 ========= SIDE WITH HANGING PAWNS:

 - pawn breakthrough, transforming them into passed pawn
 - pawn sacrifice for attack, usually attack on the king

 ========= SIDE FIGHTING HANGING PAWNS:

 - strive for exchanges
 - pressure the pawns from afar (with rooks, queen or bishops)
 - force them to move, creating backward pawn
 - attack them with the pawn from a flank
