Side with the pawn phalanx strives to move them as far as possible, resulting in driving opponent's pieces to passive positions damaging their coordination.
Consider sacrificing phalanx pawns to open files and diagonals for your attacking pieces.

Side fighting against pawn phalanx can deploy one of the following techniques (or combine them):
- pressure the pawn phalanx with the pieces from afar (usually with rooks)
- exchange one or both of the phalanx pawns, typically counter-striking phalanx with wing pawn
- force one of the phalanx pawns to advance, block them and then siege them since they are immobilised and weak
